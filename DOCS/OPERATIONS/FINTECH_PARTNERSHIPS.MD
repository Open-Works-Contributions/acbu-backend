# Fintech Partnerships

## Multi-Country Fintech Partners (Pan-African)

These operate across multiple target countries:

1. **Flutterwave** - Present in all 10 countries, strongest option
2. **Chipper Cash** - Uganda, Kenya, Rwanda, Nigeria, Ghana, Tanzania, South Africa
3. **Wave** - Côte d'Ivoire, Uganda, Kenya (Francophone focus)
4. **MTN Mobile Money** - Uganda, Rwanda, Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana (17 African countries total)
5. **Orange Money** - Côte d'Ivoire, Egypt, Morocco (18 African countries)

## Recommended Partnership Strategy

### Primary Partner (All Countries)
- **Flutterwave** - Main reserve custody and on/off-ramp partner across all 10 countries

### Secondary Partners (Regional Redundancy)
- **East Africa:** Chipper Cash
- **West Africa:** Wave + MTN Mobile Money
- **North Africa:** Fawry (Egypt), PayTech (Morocco)
- **South Africa:** Ozow

### Why Multiple Partners
- Risk diversification (no single point of failure)
- Regulatory redundancy (if one partner faces issues)
- Better liquidity across regions
- Competitive rates for users

### Ghana (GHS)
